Ever heard of energy audit? Yeah, you guessed it right, its the same drawn out process which involves lots of instruments and surveys. The process is usually expensive and if not done properly may not yield any fruitful results. This makes energy audit unsuitable for homes or small businesses. But not anymore. What we offer is a very cost effective solution for energy auditing that involves a small but power-packed hardware that collects valuable information with the ease of clicking a picture and backed with a web application that parses this data and tranforms your home online. For energy auditors an offline version of the software will also be made available.

The website will also act as an interface between energy auditors and people wanting an energy audit. The first shipment of both hardware and software will be on December 2014. The starting price of hardware will be $300 and the software will be $150.
